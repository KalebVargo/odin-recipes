Overnight Oats

Description

Here we have a deliciously easy overnight oats recipe for you. This is a incredibly balanced meal to kick start your day! Using apples and cinnamon, with a splash of maple syrup, we transform these oats from zero to hero. Join us.

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats
  • Maple Syrup
  • Dehydrated apples
  • Protein Powder
  • Oat Milk

Steps

  1. Add oats, protein powder, maple syrup, oat milk, and dehydrated apples to a jar.
  2. Shake like your life depends on it!
  3. Move to the fridge and consume the following morning.
