Fried Egg

Description

This is a recipe that reminds me of home. This is the first dish I learned to create and it's one I still make often to this day. Teach this to your kids, heck, teach this to your dog. You're gonna love it! Join me ;)

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat
  2. Melt a tablespoon of ghee into the pan
  3. Crack the egg into the hot ghee until cooked, then remove and serve
