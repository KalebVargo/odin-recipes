Dehydrated Apples

Description

Here we have dried apples using a mixture of cinnamon, cardamum, sugar, and lemon juice. These apples are a great snack or useful addition to other recipes like overnight oats, which is what I use them for!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamum
  • Lemon Juice
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Wash apples and core them.
  2. Cut apples into 1/4 inch sections
  3. Rinse off apple slices and add to gallon ziploc bag.
  4. Whisk together Sugar, Cinnamon, Cardamum, Lemon Juice, and Vanilla Extract.
  5. Add whisked mixture to the apples in the gallon ziploc bag.
  6. Once combined, let sit for 10 minutes to absorb. Then put apples on parchment paper on a tray into the oven at the lowest it will go for 3 hours.
